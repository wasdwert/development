Goals
Main goal: Bonsai master
Instructions:
Since being small you wanted to grow bonsais and be the best in it. Now is the time to do so!
Go out and built yourself up to be a bonsai master!
Goal:
Have the best bonsai possible.
Tutorial: Expeditions
Instructions:
First you need to find a seedling. Go on an "Expedition in your neighborhood".
Goal:
Obtain one seedling. Return here afterwards.
Tutorial: Plant a bonsai
Instructions:
Your first seedling, now you need to plant it. Click one of the empty bonsai spaces on the left side and plant your first bonsai.
Goal:
Plant one bonsai. Return here afterwards.
Tutorial: Bonsai species and potentials
Instructions:
One bonsai isn't enough, you're thinking to yourself. You want more tree species to grow into bonsais and maybe a seedling with a better potential of being a great bonsai.
Go back on an expedition. You can now go on longer expeditions for better chances on different tree species and potential.
Goal:
Obtain two different tree species. Return here afterwards.
Tutorial: Learn building robots
Instructions:
With all the seedlings you found, you don't know how to grow them all at the same time.
If I only would have more help, you thought.
Luckily you're handy with electronics and remember having an instruction book for building robots. You should learn how to build one.
Goal:
Learn 'Building robots'.
Tutorial: Craft a robot
Instructions:
Reading the book took some time, but finally you finished. Now it's time to build your new helper.
Go to 'Craft' and craft a robot.
Goal:
Craft a robot.
Tutorial: Bonsai growing
Instructions:
When you flick the switch, your robot beeps and looks at you.
How may I help you, he asks?
Grow together with your new robotic helper two bonsais to at least level 5.
Goal:
Two bonsais with at least level 5.
Tutorial: Compost
Instructions:
Your bonsais are growing. But it does take longer and longer, the bigger your bonsais get, you realize.
With the experience from doing expeditions and growing bonsais, I should have the skills by now to learn how to make compost and produce some, you think.
That might help them to grow quicker!
Goal:
Learn 'Compost making' and craft 3 compost.
Tutorial: Level Up Bonsai
Instructions:
With the addition of compost your bonsais are now growing quicker. Time to bring them up to the next level.
You can now add compost to bonsais when they still have to grow for 60 or more seconds, through the growing button.
Goal:
Grow a bonsai to level 10
Tutorial: Growing Skill
Instructions:
Your knowledge and skill level is slowly growing. Time to refine your skill in growing your bonsais and giving them another boost.
You can now learn "Growing #1" a skill that boosts the increase of CP with each level.
Note: CP stand for Combined Points and measure the growth of your bonsai. The higher the level and potential of a bonsai, the higher the CP can be.
Goal:
Learn "Growing #1" and grow a bonsai with 30 or more CP
Tutorial: Shopping
Instructions:
Strolling your neighborhood for new bonsais you meet a person that tells you about different bonsais on the outskirts of the city.
Time to go shopping, so that you can take an expedition there.
Note: Bonsais have type. The rarer the type the more worth the bonsai is.
Goal:
Buy the backpack
Tutorial: Shopping #2
Instructions:
Because your bonsais need more and more care you think about building another robot.
Unfortunately you don't have the resources yet, but the shop might be able to help you.
Goal:
Build a third robot
Tutorial: Shaping skill
Instructions:
One day you meet another person that grows bonsais and learn that you can not only grow bonsais, but also actively shape them.
You can now learn "Shaping #1" a skill that lets you distribute a portion of the new CP your bonsai gains each level.
Note: Your bonsais CP are calculated from the points of the four individual categories (Foliage, branches, trunk and roots). Besides your shaping skill, these are influenced through the nature of the bonsai.
Goal:
Grow a bonsai with 20 or more points in one of the individual categories
Tutorial: Patience skill
Instructions:
The longer you grow bonsais, the more you feel like being more patient in general.
You can now learn "Patience" a skill that lets you grow a bonsai over multiple level at once.
Goal:
Learn "Patience"
An open world
Instructions:
This is the current end of the game. Feel free to play further and try out the game, but there are no further tasks or mechanics to discover.
For now...
Goal:
Grow bonsais or go on more expeditions
